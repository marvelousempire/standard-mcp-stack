# Mission Standard

The mission is the durable statement of why the system exists and who benefits from its work.

## Trust lens

The mission is comparable to the declared purpose of the trust. Trustees may exercise authority only in support of that purpose.

## Business lens

The mission is the company mandate. Directors use it to decide which projects belong inside the organization and which do not.

## Required sections

- purpose
- beneficiaries
- permitted fields of operation
- excluded fields of operation
- success criteria
- escalation conditions
- amendment authority

## Planning test

Before approving a plan, a director should be able to answer:

1. Does the request serve an authorized beneficiary?
2. Does it advance the mission?
3. Is the requested work within scope?
4. Does the plan respect bylaws and authority limits?
5. Is there a safer or simpler path?
6. What evidence will show that the mission was completed?
